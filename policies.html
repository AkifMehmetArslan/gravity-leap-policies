Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

Gravity Leap is developed and published by Akif Mehmet Arslan under the brand name "LemonWorks Apps". This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights in relation to that information.

1. Data We Collect

Gravity Leap does not require a traditional account, email address, phone number, contacts, camera access, or precise location access to play.

The following data may be collected or processed:

  • Nickname and local player ID - used for player identity, nickname reservation, leaderboards, and game functionality. Nickname entry is optional, but if you choose one it may be visible to other players.
  • Leaderboard and gameplay data - scores, zones, planets crossed, combo data, boosts used, challenge results, and similar game statistics.
  • Crash logs and diagnostics - error messages, stack traces, platform, app environment, and related diagnostic details used to identify and fix bugs.
  • Anonymous or pseudonymous usage events - app opens, run starts, run completions, ad placements, leaderboard views, zone changes, and similar analytics events.
  • Advertising identifiers and ad interaction data - IDFA, GAID, impressions, clicks, frequency capping, approximate region from IP address, and fraud-prevention signals processed by Google AdMob and related Google services.
  • Purchase-related data - product IDs, purchase status, receipts, or transaction status if in-app purchases are enabled and used. We do not receive your full payment card or bank details.

2. Local Storage

Gravity Leap stores some data locally on your device, including coins, high score, best zone progress, owned skins, equipped skin, tutorial status, settings, boost inventory, and local player identifiers.

Local data is generally removed if you uninstall the app or clear the app's storage through your device settings.

3. Online Features and Leaderboards

Gravity Leap may use Firebase and related Google services to store or process online data such as leaderboard entries, nickname reservation records, analytics events, crash reports, remote configuration, and challenge or gameplay state.

Leaderboard entries may include your chosen nickname, score, zone, planets crossed, and related gameplay statistics. Please do not use your real name or sensitive personal information as your nickname.

4. Advertising

Gravity Leap displays interstitial and rewarded advertisements served by Google AdMob.

  • Rewarded ads are optional and may provide in-game rewards such as coins, boosts, or a continue option after a run.
  • Interstitial ads may be shown between gameplay sessions or after certain game events.
  • Gravity Leap requests non-personalized or contextual ads where supported. Google AdMob may still process advertising data for ad delivery, measurement, frequency capping, fraud prevention, security, and compliance.

We do not sell personal data. Advertising-related data is processed by Google AdMob in accordance with Google's own privacy policies.

5. GDPR and EEA/UK Users

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, a Google consent dialog may be shown before advertisements are loaded when required by Google AdMob or applicable law.

  • You may grant or withhold consent where consent is requested.
  • The app should continue to function even if you decline personalized advertising or tracking permission.
  • You may also control ad personalization and tracking through your device settings, Google settings, and Apple settings where available.

If you are an EEA or UK resident, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to processing of your personal data, and the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

6. iOS App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On iOS 14.5 and later, Gravity Leap may request permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework before accessing data used for tracking across apps and websites owned by other companies.

  • If you allow tracking, ads or ad measurement may use IDFA.
  • If you deny tracking, ads may still be shown, but they should not use IDFA for tracking.
  • You may change this setting anytime in iOS Settings - Privacy & Security - Tracking.

7. Children's Privacy

Gravity Leap is a general-audience game and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. Age requirements may vary by jurisdiction.

If you believe a child has provided personal information through Gravity Leap, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

8. Third-Party Services

Gravity Leap may use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase and Google Cloud services - online game data, leaderboards, nickname reservation, analytics, crash reports, diagnostics, and remote configuration.
  • Google AdMob - advertising, rewarded ads, ad measurement, and fraud prevention.
  • Google Play services and Apple services - platform features, app distribution, payments where enabled, and security.
  • Expo services - build, update, and runtime infrastructure where applicable.

9. Data Retention

Local progress and settings remain on your device until removed by you, overwritten by the app, or deleted when you uninstall the app or clear app storage.

Server-side data such as leaderboard entries, nickname reservation records, analytics events, and crash reports may be retained as long as necessary to provide game features, maintain security, improve the game, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and prevent abuse.

10. Data Deletion Requests

You may request deletion of server-side data associated with your Gravity Leap player profile by emailing: lemonworksapps@gmail.com

Please use the subject line Gravity Leap Data Deletion Request and include your in-game nickname and any details that help us identify your player profile. We may ask for additional information to verify the request.

After verification, we will delete or anonymize applicable server-side data unless retention is required for legal, security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or platform compliance reasons.

11. Data Security

Data transmitted between the app and third-party services is generally encrypted in transit using TLS. We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

12. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Restrict or object to processing.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
  • Request data portability where applicable.
  • Opt out of sale or sharing where applicable.

13. California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act and California Privacy Rights Act, including the right to request information about personal data collected and the right to request deletion. We do not sell personal information.
